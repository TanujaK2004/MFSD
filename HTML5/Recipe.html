Masala Dosa Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 cup chana dal (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or ghee for cooking
  • 2 boiled potatoes
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Curry leaves

Steps to Prepare

  1. Soak rice and urad dal overnight, then grind into a smooth batter.
  2. Ferment the batter for 6–8 hours until fluffy.
  3. Prepare the potato filling: heat oil, add mustard seeds, curry leaves, onions, chilies, and turmeric. Add boiled potatoes and mash well.
  4. Heat a non-stick pan, pour a ladle of batter, and spread thinly to form a dosa.
  5. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until golden brown.
  6. Place the potato filling inside, fold the dosa, and serve hot.
