IDOL PROFILE

STEVE JOBS

Steve Jobs (1955–2011) was the co-founder and former CEO of Apple Inc. and a visionary entrepreneur who revolutionized personal computing, smartphones, music, and digital media.
He helped create iconic products like the Macintosh, iPod, iPhone, and iPad, and co-founded Pixar, transforming animation.
Known for his design perfectionism and innovative thinking, Jobs left a lasting impact on technology and culture worldwide.
